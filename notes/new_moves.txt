Armored Charge

Move in a line of hexes up to your SPE. Roll Carapace + STR. Each insect in that line must roll SPE + SR to resist. Each insect you succeed against gains Wounds and is pushed 1 for each net success.

Brittle touch

Roll Chill + TR against an enemy's Carapace + SR. The target gains wounds and Chilled for each net success.

Ground Slam

Land on the ground beneath you and roll STR + Flight. Each insect within a number of hexes equal to your successes must roll SPE + SR to resist, taking one wound for each net success.

Heat Exchange

Touch an adjacent insect and roll Heat + TR vs. SPE + SR. The target gains Burns equal to the number of net successes.

Water Pistol

Roll Hydro + TR vs. STR + SR against an insect up to 5 hexes away. The insect gains Wounds and is pushed 1 for each net success.

Planned Strike

Roll KEN + TR vs. KEN + SR against an adjacent insect. The target gains wounds for each net success. This move ignores Carapace.

Focused Light

Roll Light + TR vs. SPE against a target up to 5 hexes away. The target gains Wounds and Blind equal to the number of net successes.

Fury

Roll PAS + TR + Current Wounds vs. STR + SR against an adjacent insect. The target gains wounds for each net success. On a success, advance self one.

Infestation

Roll Plants + TR vs. STR + SR against an insect up to 4 hexes away. The target gains wounds and infected equal to the number of net successes.

Mind Bite

Roll KEN + Psycho vs. KEN + PAS against an insect up to 10 hexes away. The target gains wounds and Confused equal to the number of net successes.

Hit and Run

Roll SPE + TR vs. SPE + SR against an adjacent insect. The target gains wounds for each net success. On a success, you move up to your SPE.

Two Pole Strike

Roll Static + TR vs. SPE + Static against two target insects each up to 3 hexes away. The targets gain wounds and Shocked for each net success.

Reckless Bash.

Roll Max Wounds + TR vs. STR + SR against an adjacent insect. The target gains wounds for each net success, and you gain 1 wound for every three net successes.

Hercules Toss

Roll STR + TR vs. STR + SR against an adjacent insect. The target gains wounds and is pushed 1 for each net success. If the insect collides with another insect, both take 1 wound.

Autothysis

Sacrifice a number of Swarms. Roll Swarms + TR + the number of sacrifices and choose a hex up to 4 hexes away. Each insect within one hex of the chosen hex rolls SPE + SR, gaining wounds for each net success.

Venom Sting

Roll Venom + TR vs. SR against an adjacent insect. The target gains Wounds and Infected for each net success.

Vibro Wave

Roll PAS + Vibe vs. PAS against an insect up to 3 hexes away. The target gains wounds and Stunned for each net success.

Web Shot

Roll WEB + TR vs. SPE against an insect up to 4 hexes away. The target gains wounds and Immobilised for each net success.
