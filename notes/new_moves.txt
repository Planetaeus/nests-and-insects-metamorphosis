Armored Charge

Move in a line of hexes up to your SPE. Roll Carapace + STR. Each insect in that line must roll SPE + SR to resist. Each insect you succeed against gains Wounds and is pushed 1 for each net success.

Brittle touch

Roll Chill + TR vs. Carapace + SR against adjacent insect. The insect gains Wounds and Chilled for each net success.

Umbra

Roll Darkness + TR vs KEN + SR against adjacent insect. The insect gains Wounds and Blind for each net success.

Ground Slam

Land on the ground beneath you and roll STR + Flight. Each insect within a number of hexes equal to your successes must roll SPE + SR to resist, taking one Wound for each net success.

Heat Exchange

Roll Heat + TR vs. SPE + SR against adjacent insect. The insect gains Burns equal to the number of net successes.

Water Pistol

Roll Hydro + TR vs. STR + SR against an insect up to 5 hexes away. The insect gains Wounds and is pushed 1 for each net success.

Planned Strike

Roll KEN + TR vs. KEN + SR against adjacent insect. The insect gains Wounds for each net success. This move ignores Carapace.

Focused Light

Roll Light + TR vs. SPE + SR against a insect up to 5 hexes away. The insect gains Wounds and Blind equal to the number of net successes.

Fury

Roll PAS + TR + Current Wounds vs. STR + SR against an adjacent insect. The insect gains Wounds for each net success. On a success, advance self 1.

Infestation

Roll Plants + TR vs. STR + SR against an insect up to 4 hexes away. The insect gains Wounds and infected equal to the number of net successes.

Mind Bite

Roll KEN + Psycho vs. KEN + PAS against an insect up to 10 hexes away. The insect gains Wounds and Confused equal to the number of net successes.

Hit and Run

Roll SPE + TR vs. SPE + SR against an adjacent insect. The insect gains Wounds for each net success. On a success, you move up to your SPE.

Two Pole Strike

Roll Static + TR vs. SPE + Static against two insects each up to 3 hexes away. The insects gain Wounds and Shocked for each net success.

Reckless Bash.

Roll Max Wounds + TR vs. STR + SR against an adjacent insect. The insect gains Wounds for each net success, and you gain 1 Wound for every three net successes.

Hercules Toss

Roll STR + TR vs. STR + SR against an adjacent insect. The insect gains Wounds and is pushed 1 for each net success. If the insect collides with another insect, both take 1 Wound.

Autothysis

Sacrifice a number of Swarms. Roll Swarms + TR + the number of sacrifices and choose a hex up to 4 hexes away. Each insect within one hex of the chosen hex rolls SPE + SR, gaining wounds for each net success.

Venom Sting

Roll Venom + TR vs. STR + SR against an adjacent insect. The insect gains Wounds and Poisoned for each net success.

Vibro Wave

Roll PAS + Vibe vs. PAS + SR against an insect up to 3 hexes away. The insect gains Wounds and Stunned for each net success.

Web Shot

Roll WEB + TR vs. SPE + SR against an insect up to 4 hexes away. The insect gains Wounds and Immobilised for each net success.
