 5.3.0.0 Attack Descriptions
  
  Armor Bash
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Carapace 1
  Roll STR + Carapace vs. STR + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. For each Net Success, the target gains Wounds and an adjacent Ally gains a positive modifier to her next Attack Resistance Roll.
  
  Armored Charge
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Carapace 1
  Move in a line of Hexes up to your SPE. Roll SPE + Carapace. Each Insect in that line must roll SPE + Carapace to Resist, then gains Wounds and is Pushed 1 for each Net Success.
  
  Armored Spikes
  Range: Special
  Requirements: Carapace 1
  The next time you are Missed by a Close Attack before your next turn, the Attacker gains Wounds and Bleeding for each Net Failure
  
  Assuréd Strike
  Range: Close
  Requirements: KEN 7
  Roll KEN against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds for each Net Success.
  
  Autothysis
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Swarming 1
  Spend one Swarms and Roll KEN + Swarming and choose a Hex within Swarming Hexes. Each Insect within one Hex rolls SPE to Resist, then gains Wounds and Immobile for each Net Success.
  
  Bandage
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Web 1, Healing 1
  Roll Web + Healing vs. STR against an adjacent Ally. For each Net Success, target heals Wounds and reduces Bleeding.
  
  Bolster
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Leadership 1
  Roll PAS + Leadership vs. PAS + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds for each Net Success. Then each Ally within Leadership Hexes Rolls as if Resisting a Rally Action, then Advances appropriately.
  
  Bright Beam
  Range: Ranged
  Requirements: Light 1
  Roll PAS + Light vs. PAS + Light + Carapace against an Insect within twice Light Hexes. The target gains Wounds and Blind for each Net Success.
  
  Burning Blaze
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Heat 1
  Roll PAS + Heat. Each Insect within Successes Hexes Rolls SPE + Heat + Carapace to Resist, then gains Wounds and Burns for each Net Success.
  
  Burning Blow
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Heat 1
  Roll STR + Heat vs. PAS + Heat + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds and Burns for each Net Success.
  
  Chilling Blast
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Chill 1
  Choose a Hex within Chill Hexes. Roll KEN + Chill. Each Insect within one Hex rolls STR + Chill to Resist, then gains Wounds and Brittle for each Net Success.
  
  Chilling Blow
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Chill 1
  Roll SPE + Chill vs. KEN + Chill + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds and Brittle for each Net Success.
  
  Chitin Catapult
  Range: Ranged
  Requirements: Carapace 1
  Roll KEN + Carapace vs. SPE against an Insect within Carapace Hexes. The target gains Wounds and Brittle for each Net Success. You gain Brittle 2.
  
  Corrosive Sacrifice
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Swarming 1, Toxic 1
  Spend two Swarms and Roll Swarming + Toxic and choose a Hex within Swarming Hexes. Each Insect within one Hex rolls SPE + Carapace + Toxic to Resist, then gains Wounds, Immobile, and Brittle for each Net Success.
  
  Envenomed Spikes
  Range: Special
  Requirements: Carapace 1, Toxic 1
  Add Toxic as a positive modifier to your next Resistance Roll against a Close Attack before your next turn. If the Attack Misses, the Attacker gains Wounds and Poisoned for each Net Failure.
  
  Flaming Web
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Heat 1, Web 1
  Choose a Hex within Web Hexes and Roll Heat + Web. Each Insect within Successes Hexes Rolls SPE + Heat + Carapace to Resist, then gains Wounds, Burns, and Immobile equal to the Net Successes.
  
  Flythrough
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Flight 1
  Roll SPE + Flight vs. STR + Carapace + Flight against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds for each Net Success. Move Flight Hexes.
  
  Fontanellar Bomb
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Hydro 1
  Roll PAS + Hydro and choose a Hex within Hydro Hexes. Each Insect within Successes Hexes Rolls STR + Hydro + Carapace to Resist, then gains Wounds, Reduces Burns, and is Pushed 1 from chosen Hex for each Net Success.
  
  Fontanellar Gun
  Range: Ranged
  Requirements: Hydro 1
  Choose an Insect within Hydro Hexes. Roll KEN + Hydro vs. SPE + Hydro. The target gains Wounds, Reduces Burns, and is Pushed 1 for each Net Success.
  
  Fontanellar Vortex
  Range: Ranged
  Requirements: Hydro 1
  Roll STR + Hydro. Each Insect of your choice within Hydro Hexes Rolls STR + Hydro + Carapace to Resist, then gains wounds, Reduces Burns, and is Pushed 1 for each Net Success.
  
  Fungal Bungle
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Plant 1
  Roll STR + Plant vs. STR + Plant + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds and Infected for each Net Success.
  
  Fury
  Range: Close
  Requirements: PAS 5
  Roll PAS + Current Wounds vs. STR + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds for each Net Success. On a Success, Advance 1.
  
  Ground Slam
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Flight 1
  Fly Flight Hexes and land on the ground beneath. Roll STR + Flight. Each Insect within Successes Hexes must roll SPE + Carapace + Flight to Resist, gaining Wounds for each Net Success.
  
  Gust
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Flight 1
  Roll PAS + Flight. Each Insect in a line within Flight Hexes Rolls STR + Carapace + Flight to Resist, then gains Wounds and is Pushed 1 for each Net Success.
  
  Healing Dose
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Healing 1, Toxic 1
  Roll Toxic + Healing vs. PAS against an adjacent Insect. The target heals Wounds for each Net Success.
  
  Healing Honey
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Healing 1, Plant 1
  Roll Plant + Healing vs. STR against an adjacent Insect. The target heals Wounds for each Net Success.
  
  Hercules Toss
  Range: Close
  Requirements: STR 7
  Roll STR vs. STR + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds and is Pushed 1 in any direction for each Net Success. If the Insect collides with another Insect or an Obstacle before it stops moving, the Insect(s) gain Wounds equal to the Hexes not yet moved, and the Insect stops moving.
  
  Hit and Run
  Range: Close
  Requirements: SPE 1
  Roll SPE vs. SPE + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. For each Net Success, the target gains Wounds and you may move that many Hexes.
  
  Hot-othysis
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Heat 1, Swarming 1
  Spend two Swarms and Roll Swarming + Heat and choose a Hex within Swarming Hexes. Each Insect within one Hex rolls SPE + Heat to Resist, gaining Wounds and Burns for each Net Success.
  
  Inflict Stillness
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Toxic 1
  Roll PAS + Toxic vs. STR + Toxic + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds and Paralysed for each Net Success.
  
  Line of Lightning
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Static 1
  Choose two Insects within Static Hexes and Roll PAS + Static. Chosen Insects and each Insect in a line between them Rolls PAS + Static + Carapace, then gains Wounds and Shocks for each Net Success.
  
  Mandible Crush
  Range: Close
  Requires: Strength 1
  Roll STR vs. STR + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds and Immobile for each Net Success.
  
  Overwhelm
  Range: Area
  Requirement: Swarming 1
  Spend two Swarms and Roll PAS + Swarming and choose a Hex within Swarming Hexes. Each Insect within one Hex rolls PAS + Carapace to Resist, then gains Wounds and Recoils for each Net Success.
  
  Plannéd Strike
  Range: Close
  Requirements: KEN 5
  Roll KEN vs. KEN against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds for each Net Success.
  
  Point-Blank Flare
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Light 1
  Roll PAS + Light vs. PAS + Light + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds and Blind for each Net Success.
  
  Polar Discharge
  Range: Ranged
  Requirements: Static 1
  Roll KEN + Static vs. PAS + Static against two Insects within Static Hexes. Each target gains Wounds and Shocks for each Net Success.
  
  Psychic Mandibles
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Psycho 1
  Roll KEN + Psycho vs. KEN + Psycho against an Insect within Psycho Hexes. The target gains Wounds and Confused for each Net Success.
  
  Rhinoceros Gore
  Range: Close
  Requirements:
  Move in a line of Hexes up to your SPE. Roll SPE + STR. Each Insect in that line Rolls SPE + Carapace to Resist, then gains Wounds and Bleeding for each Net Success.
  
  Reinforced Silk
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Carapace 1, Web 1
  Roll Carapace + Web. Each Insect within one Hex Rolls SPE + Carapace + Web to Resist. Enemies gain Wounds and Immobile for each Net Success. Allies Reduce Bleeding and gain a positive modifier to their next Attack Resistance for each Net Success.
  
  Rout
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Signaling 1
  Roll PAS + Signaling vs. PAS + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds for each Net Success. Each Enemy within Signaling Hexes Rolls as if Resisting an Intimidate Action, then Recoils appropriately.
  
  Sap Trap
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Plant 1, Web 1
  Roll Plant + Web. Each Insect within one Hex Rolls STR + Plant + Web to Resist, then gains Wounds, Immobile, and Infected for each Net Success.
  
  Searing Shine
  Range: Ranged
  Requirements: Heat 1, Light 1
  Roll Heat + Light vs. Heat + Light + Carapace against an Insect within twice Light Hexes. The target gains Wounds, Blind, and Burns for each Net Success.
  
  Slicer
  Range: Close
  Requirements:
  Roll SPE vs. SPE + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds and Bleeding for each Net Success.
  
  Static Crunch
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Static 1
  Roll STR + Static vs. PAS + Static + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds and Shocks for each Net Success.
  
  Supreme Speed
  Range: Close
  Requirements: SPE 7
  Roll SPE vs. SPE + Carapace against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds for each Net Success. On a Success, make another attack against an adjacent Insect (this can be the same as the original target).
  
  Swarming Call
  Range: Special
  Requirements: Swarming 1
  Roll Swarming. On Partial/Total/Critical Success, regain 1/2/3 Swarms.
  
  Toxic Sting
  Range: Close
  Requirements: Toxic 1
  Roll KEN + Toxic vs. KEN + Toxic against an adjacent Insect. The target gains Wounds and Poisoned for each Net Success.
  
  Twister
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Flight 1
  Choose a Hex within Flight Hexes. Roll STR + Flight. Each Insect within one Hex Rolls STR + Flight + Carapace to Resist, then gains Wounds and is pushed 1 for each Net Success.
  
  Vibral Assault
  Range: Ranged
  Requirements: Vibe 1
  Roll PAS + Vibe vs. PAS + Vibe + Carapace against an Insect within Vibe Hexes. The target gains Wounds and Paralysed for each Net Success.
  
  Vine Entwine
  Range: Ranged
  Requirements: Plant 1
  Roll STR + Plant vs. STR + Plant + Carapace against an Insect within Plant Hexes. The target gains Wounds and is Pushed 1 towards you for each Net Success.
  
  Web Assault
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Web 1
  Roll SPE + Web and choose a Hex within Web Hexes. Each Insect within one Hex Rolls SPE + Web + Carapace to Resist. Enemies gain Wounds and Immobile for each Net Success. Allies reduce Bleeding for each Net Success.
  
  Web Shot
  Range: Ranged
  Requirements: Web 1
  Roll KEN + Web vs. SPE + Web against an Insect within Web Hexes. If an enemy, target gains Wounds and Immobile for each Net Success. If an ally, target reduces Bleeding for each Net Success.
  
  Willéd Strike
  Range: Area
  Requirements: Psycho 1
  Roll KEN + Psycho. Each Enemy within Psycho Hexes Rolls KEN + Psycho to Resist, then gains Wounds and Charmed for each Net Success.
